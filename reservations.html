House Rules

  1. Entry & Security Checks
    All customers will be searched upon entry using metal detector wands.
    Any prohibited items found will result in denied entry.
    Last entry times:
    Sunday to Thursday: 01:15 AM
    Friday & Saturday: 02:15 AM
    No re-entry once you have left the venue.

  2. Age & ID Verification
    We are strictly an over-18 venue – proof of age will be required.
    After 10 PM, all customers must present valid photo ID.
    We operate Challenge 25 – if you appear under 25, you must provide valid ID.
    IDs will be scanned via our ClubScan system.

  3. Zero Tolerance Policy
    We have a strict zero-tolerance policy on drugs.
    Any drug-related activity will result in removal and reporting to authorities.
    Aggressive or anti-social behaviour will result in removal and a ban.

  4. CCTV Monitoring & Security
    CCTV is in operation throughout the premises.
    Footage may be shared with authorities upon request.

  5. Conduct & Respect for Others
    Be kind and polite to all staff and fellow customers.
    Customers must leave in an orderly manner.
    Management may ask guests to leave earlier than closing time for safety reasons.
    Loud or disruptive behaviour will not be tolerated.

  6. Dress Code & Venue Behaviour
    Smart casual attire is required. No sportswear or excessively casual wear.
    Loud, disruptive, or unruly behaviour is not allowed.

  7. Alcohol & Substance Regulations
    Outside food or drink is prohibited.
    Illegal drug use is strictly banned.

  8. Weapons & Dangerous Items
    Weapons or hazardous items are strictly prohibited.

  9. 90 Minutes Policy
    During peak hours, a 90-minute seating limit applies.

  10. Management & Security Rights
    Management and security may refuse entry or service.

  11. Final Notice
    If you do not agree to the house rules, please do not enter.

Privacy Policy

Effective Date: 10/04/2025

1. About Us
We are dedicated to providing exceptional dining experiences while respecting your privacy. This policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your personal information when you visit our restaurant, interact with us online, or use our services.


2. What Information We Collect
We may collect the following types of personal data:

  • Reservations & Bookings: Name, contact details, and any special requests.
  • Transactions: Payment details, receipts, and billing information.
  • Website & Digital Interactions: IP addresses, cookies, and browsing behaviour.
  • Marketing Preferences: Information provided when subscribing to newsletters or promotions.
  • Feedback & Communication: Records of interactions via phone, email, or social media.
  • Wi-Fi Usage: If you use our restaurant’s Wi-Fi, we may collect device information and browsing activity in accordance with our terms of service.

Please note that phone calls may be recorded for training and quality assurance purposes.


3. How We Use Your Information

  • Process reservations and orders efficiently.
  • Communicate promotions, updates, and special offers (with consent).
  • Improve our services and enhance your experience.
  • Ensure security and fraud prevention.
  • Comply with legal and regulatory requirements.
  • Provide access to our Wi-Fi services and ensure network security.

4. Legal Basis for Processing Personal Data
We process personal data in accordance with the UK GDPR and the Data Protection Act 2018. Legal bases include:

  • Performance of a Contract: To provide requested services such as reservations and orders.
  • Legitimate Interests: For operational improvements, fraud prevention, and customer experience enhancement.
  • Consent: Where required for marketing communications or optional data collection.
  • Legal Obligations: To comply with applicable laws and regulations.

5. Sharing & Protecting Your Data
We do not sell your data. However, we may share it with:

  • Payment providers for transaction processing.
  • Service providers assisting in restaurant operations.
  • Authorities when required by law.
  • Third-party platforms that help us manage bookings, promotions, and customer feedback.

We take reasonable security measures to protect your data from unauthorised access, loss, or disclosure, in line with UK GDPR requirements.


6. Your Rights Under GDPR
Under UK GDPR, you have the following rights regarding your personal data:

  • Access: Request a copy of the personal data we hold about you.
  • Correction: Ask us to rectify inaccurate or incomplete information.
  • Deletion: Request the removal of your personal data where applicable.
  • Restriction: Limit how we process your data under certain circumstances.
  • Objection: Opt out of certain processing activities, such as direct marketing.
  • Data Portability: Request a transfer of your personal data in a structured, commonly used format.

To exercise these rights, contact us at: info@tigerbayshisha.co.uk


7. Cookies & Tracking Technologies
We use cookies to enhance your experience. You can manage cookie preferences through your browser settings.


8. Updates to This Policy
We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. Changes will be posted on our website, and continued use of our services implies acceptance of the revised policy.


9. Contact Us
If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, or how we handle your data, please contact us at: info@tigerbayshisha.co.uk
