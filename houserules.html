House Rules

  1. Entry & Security Checks
    • All customers will be searched upon entry using metal detector wands.
    • Any prohibited items found will result in denied entry.
    • Last entry times:
      • Sunday to Thursday: 01:15 AM
      • Friday & Saturday: 02:15 AM
    • No re-entry once you have left the venue.
  2. Age & ID Verification
    • We are strictly an over-18 venue – proof of age will be required.
    • After 10 PM, all customers must present physical valid photo identification.
    • We operate Challenge 25 – if you appear under 25, you must provide valid ID.
    • IDs will be scanned through our ClubScan system for verification.
  3. Zero Tolerance Policy
    • We have a strict zero-tolerance policy on drugs.
    • Any drug-related activity or possession will result in immediate removal and reporting to the relevant authorities.
    • Aggressive or anti-social behaviour will result in removal and a permanent ban on future entry.
  4. CCTV Monitoring & Security
    • CCTV is in operation throughout the premises for the safety of customers and staff.
    • Footage may be shared with the authorities upon request.
  5. Conduct & Respect for Others
    • Be kind and polite to all staff and fellow customers.
    • Customers must leave in an orderly manner, showing respect for our neighbours and local community.
    • Management may ask customers to leave earlier than closing time for crowd control and neighbourhood considerations.
    • Loud, disruptive or unruly behaviour in the venue will not be tolerated.
  6. Dress Code & Venue Behaviour
    • Smart casual attire is required; no sportswear or excessively casual clothing.
    • Loud, disruptive, or unruly behaviour will not be tolerated.
  7. Alcohol & Substance Regulations
    • Bringing outside food or drink is prohibited.
    • Drug use and possession of illegal substances are strictly banned.
  8. Weapons & Dangerous Items
    • Possession of weapons or hazardous items is strictly prohibited.
  9. Management & Security Rights
    • Management reserves the right to refuse entry or service at their discretion.
    • Security personnel have full authority to enforce all house rules.
